Hi! I’m Pooja

I’m a research engineer at Neurospin, CEA Paris-Saclay / Inria - MIND working under the supervision of Philippe Ciuciu. My research focuses on Image Analysis (Hyperspectral and Medical imaging) for different application ranging from Space to Medical Sector having my bachelor & master studies major in Computer Vision & Deep Learning. .

Previously, I did my master thesis on Multi contrast MRI reconstruction using deep unrolled neural network and currently working on to develop SPARKLING (non-cartesian sampling) for Very Low Field MRI (VLFMRI - 8mT) for premature baby.

Over the period, I have acquired expertise to do interdisciplinary research comprising Compressed Sensing, Inverse Problems, Applied Mathematics, MRI Physics, Imaging, CV & DL.
